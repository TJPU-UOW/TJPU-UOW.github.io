About
TJPU-UOW Lab. provides open develop environment for students who with a strong interest in computer vision and robotics, the opportunity to expose to a breadth of knowledge in the field with the goal of leveraging AI for social good. Students engage with faculty, staff and researchers who works in the School of Electronics and Information Engineering.
